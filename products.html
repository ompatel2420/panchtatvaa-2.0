FL 100 - The First Harvesting Filter.

FL100 is a device designed to filter rainwater collected from rooftops or other surfaces during rainfall. It is commonly used in rainwater harvesting systems to ensure that the collected water is free from debris, contaminants, and other impurities, making it suitable for various non-potable uses.

Salient Features of FL 100

  • Suitable upto roof area : 110 sqmrts
  • Max Intenstiy of Rainfall : 75 mm/hr
  • Inlet : 90 mm
  • Clean Water Outlet: 63 mm
  • Capacity: 105 LPM
  • Filter element : SS-304 Screen
  • Drain Outlet : 90 mm
  • Working Principle: Cohesive and Centrifugal Force
  • Operating Pressure : Less than 2 feet of head
  • Mesh Size : 250 microns
  • Housing : High Density Polyethylene
  • Efficiency of Filter : Above 90%
  • Source of Power : Gravity
